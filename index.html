1/2 - Installation

  1. Step 1
    Open Google Chrome and navigate to the extensions page. You can do this by clicking on the three-dot icon in the top-right corner of the browser, then selecting "More tools" and "Extensions" from the dropdown menu.
  2. Step 2
    Once you're on the extensions page, make sure the "Developer mode" toggle in the top-right corner is turned on.
  3. Step 3
    Drag the .crx file you downloaded earlier onto the extensions page. A confirmation dialog will appear, asking if you want to install the extension. Click "Add extension" to confirm.
  4. Step 4
    The extension should now be installed and visible on the extensions page. You can manage the extension's settings and permissions by clicking on the "Details" button.
  5. Step 5
    Pin the extention.

2/2 - Usage

  1. Step 1
    Click the extension icon in your extention bar.
  2. Step 2
    Check which filters you want to use.
  3. Step 4
    Type what you want to search into the search bar and click search.
